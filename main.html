The Youth Program aims to create a medium that will allow our youth to balance their Islamic values while facing the challenges of today's environment.
The program was started by a group of parents, and had its humble beginnings in the Spring of 2011 in the home of one of the parents, and Alhamdulillah in November 2011 moved into it's current location at Masjid Al-Huda, where it now benefits many Junior High and High School youth from our community.
Objectives of the program:
  • Gain practical knowledge of Islam from a qualified teacher in a fun environment.
  • Provide our youth with a big brother / big sister mentor figure.
  • Strenghten the brotherhood and sisterhood bond among our youth through sports and other team building activities.
  • Islamically guide and personally help our youth on issues they face in life.
  • Program Days : Wednesdays and Fridays for boys, Fridays for girls
  • Program Timings : 5:00 – 6:00 pm
  • Program Fees : $30 per month for boys, $15 per month for girls
  • Instructors for boys : Mawlana Asif Uddin (Age 13 and above), Brother Farhan Ahmed (Age 9 to 12)
  • Instructor for girls : Sister Sabaahath Latifi
